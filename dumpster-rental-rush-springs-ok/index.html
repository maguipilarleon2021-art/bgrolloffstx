Frequently Asked Questions

1. How do I know what size container my sensitive project needs?

Trust your intuition, and let us help. There is no pressure. Tell us about the space you are working with. A 10 yard dumpster is perfect for a single room or a pre-move purge. A 20 yard dumpster is better for a full house or an estate. We can find the size that feels right for you.

2. I’m worried about my neighbors. Is the dumpster going to be a huge eyesore?

We are very mindful of the energy we bring into a neighborhood. Our roll off containers are exceptionally clean and well-maintained. We see them as professional tools, and we keep them in a condition that reflects a deep respect for you and your sanctuary.

3. What if the emotional work of my cleanout takes longer than I thought?

The soul works on its own timeline. We honor that. Our standard rental period is spacious to allow for this. If you need more time to move through your process, we offer simple and flexible extensions. There is no rush.

4. What is the most important thing I can do to prepare my space for the dumpster’s arrival?

Create an intention. Before we arrive, take a moment. Light some sage, play some calming music, or simply state aloud what you are intending to release. This small ritual can infuse the entire process with a sense of sacred purpose.

5. I have items that are emotionally charged, but I know I need to let them go. Any advice?

Be gentle with yourself. This is the heart of the work. For these items, you might create a small ritual of release. Thank the object for the story it held for you. Acknowledge the memory. And then, when you are ready, place it in the vessel. The dumpster is strong enough to hold it.
